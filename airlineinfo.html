AIRLINE INFORMATION

Interested in learning about how we came to be and how we do it different? Read this page to learn more about HomesteadAirlines.

Our Backstory

Homestead Airlines was founded out of an attempt to experience first-class for economy pricing. Expensive airlines often charge tons of money for a flying experience that was mediocre at best, whereas discount airlines offer cramped spaces, terrible customer service, lost baggage, and worst of all, hidden fees.

Our Mission

With Homestead Airlines, you will never have to worry about losing baggage, sitting in constricted seating, and overall abysmal customer service, both in flight and out of flight. Additionally, we prioritize transparency, ensuring that no hidden fees will come up ruining your vacation budget.

Our Location

Here at Homestead Airlines, we stay true to our roots in San Francisco, California. While our flights may be centered around California, we offer flights all around the West Coast, allowing you to fly from the Los Angeles to Las Vegas to Seattle in one vacation.
