FREQUENT FLYER PROGRAM

Homestead Airlines' HomePlus program is a program that awards flyers HomePlus points depending on their miles flown with us. Mileage with Homestead Airlines will allow you to move up tiers, which unlocks perks for future flights. The HomePlus points can be redeemed for miscellaneous benefits, including upgrades in flight class, free nights at hotels, and more.

First Bag Free

Our frequent flyers always get one free check in bag, no matter the weight or size.

Priority Check In

Skip the queues right from the start with our dedicated Frequent Flyer check-in counters.

In-Flight Amenities

Enjoy numerous amenities in the air including complimentary drinks, snacks, and premium service in the cabin.
